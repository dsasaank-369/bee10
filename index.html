A Blockchain-Based Exchange to facilitate various earning and fundraising opportunities to both Companies and Investors through Public Offerings. Combination of Stock, Crypto & Forex Exchange, And NFT Marketplace. 3 Offerings every 2 Months.

Revenue Model:

  • 1. Listening Fee: We will be taking $100 Million as a Listening fee from Companies to Offer IPO and ICO.
  • 2. Stake in ICO: Will take 1% Stake in ICO of a Company at the time of Offering.
